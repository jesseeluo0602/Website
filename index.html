El Concilio of San Mateo County

El Concilio is a non-profit coalition of organizations and individuals committed to improving the quality of life for underserved communities by increasing leadership, education, employment opportunities, and access to quality and appropriate healthcare in San Mateo County.

Make a change!
El Concilio of San Mateo County (ECSMC) was incorporated in 1980 to assist and unite Latino organizations in San Mateo County.

Since the completion of a Needs Assessment Report in 1993, ECSMC partnered with the Health Services Agency to create the Nuestro Canto de Salud Coalition. This Coalition was formed to address the health needs of communities of color, in particular Latinos who reside in San Mateo County. El Concilioâ€™s Nuestro Canto de Salud Project has worked for nearly 7 years to implement accessible, culturally sensitive and linguistically appropriate health programs in the community that are linked to quality health care. The goal of Nuestro Canto de Salud is to reduce the incidence of chronic disease among Latinos in the Latino community. The project accomplishes its goals by establishing collaborative partnerships between the Latino community and general health and human service providers. These partnerships create mutually beneficial opportunities for health and human education and access to services for Latinos in San Mateo County.

Accomplishments

From January 1996 through February 2001, multi-disciplinary teams comprised of ECSMC Promotores, Community Health Workers, County Nurse Educators, and dieticians have:
• contacted over 30,000 residents through community outreach
• educated/screened 13,219 at-risk individuals for chronic conditions
• case-managed more than 1,200 residents with serious medical conditions
